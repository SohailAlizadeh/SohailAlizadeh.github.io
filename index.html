Afghanistan

Herat

Herat is the third most populous city in Afghanistan and the capital of Herat Province in the west of the country.
As an industrial, cultural, and artistic hub, the city is located on the banks of the Harirud River and has a rich history
(from the time of Alexander the Great to the Timurids) [1, 3, 4]. With attractions such as the Herat Citadel and the Grand Mosque,
Herat was a strategic city on the Silk Road and is known for its Islamic historical monuments.
